Vivek Singh Memorial Scholarship

Project Heads:

  • Mr. Nimit Goenka(nimitgoenka@vsmemorialtrust.org)

  • Mr. Vaibhav Singh (vaibhavsingh@vsmemorialtrust.org)

Vivek Singh Memorial Trust has introduced a need cum merit based annual scholarship since 2010 with a holistic approach, to the students of NIT Durgapur who are pursuing Under Graduation degrees in engineering. The Scholarship is awarded in the memory of Late Mr. Vivek Singh, who got admitted in year 2005-06 as an undergraduate student in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering in this institute but could not complete his degree as he lost his life on 29th March, 2009 while saving his batch mate from an accident. The scholarship is aimed at awarding sincere students having weak financial background.
The Scholarship will be awarded to one student for the year 2014-15 with an amount of 15,000 INR.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The Scholarship is open to all undergraduate students, except first year students.

  • Family income of the applicant must not exceed INR 4,00,000 per annum.

  • The applicant must have scored a minimum of 60% in secondary in all subjects and


    higher secondary examinations in scientific subjects - Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.

  • The applicant must have scored at least 7.0 CGPA.

  • The applicant having any other scholarship from any other sources is not eligible.

  • The applicant must have cleared all the college semester examinations, taken until the submission of


    scholarship form, in the first attempt.

  • The applicant must not have any official record of indiscipline or any other offence.

  • The applicant can avail this scholarship only once.

Process

A student has to undergo through two stages viz. evaluation stage (via application form) and interview stage. Forms will be evaluated on the basis of academic-cum-extracurricular activities and given subjective questions.

  • Duly-filled application forms must be submitted by April 13, 2014.

  • The list of shortlisted applicants for Interview will be notified on April 16, 2014.
